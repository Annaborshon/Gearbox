# headings
we use a hashtag to format headings.
there are 6 types of headings denoted by the number of hashtags;
# heading 1
## heading 2
### heading 3
#### heading 4
##### heading 5
###### heading 6

* item 1
* item 2
* item 2a

1. item 1
2. item 2
* item 2a
*hello*
**hello**
*hello* **hello**

first header | second header
-------------| -------------
content cell 1 | content cell 2
content column 1 | content column 2
